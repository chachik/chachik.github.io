What Sets us Apart

  • Specialized for Startups

    Our mindset, skills and approach are fully optimized for start-ups. By working together with you we move fast and break things. We believe that 'done' is better than 'perfect'. We craft every release as a micro MVP and use open source solutions. We entirely concentrate on your needs and specifics.

  • Accurate Perception

    We understand you. When you ask to make a bird, we don't drive you crazy with a thousand questions regarding its size, weight, speed, color, etc. Instead, we create a reasonable bird and change everything else on the fly.

  • Experience and Proficiency

    With each passing year, a single developer becomes able to produce much more value because of an increasing maturity of available tools, frameworks and libraries. That said, in order to leverage the maximum power of state-of-the-art te chnologies, engineers have to be true experts. And so our teams consist only of senior experienced engineers.

  • Lean Development

    As we are a team of experts, we can eliminate various chores, routines and management efforts. That means that we have more time to deliver you true value.

  • Curious and Self Challenging

    Taking inspiration from the book Start-up Nation, we are building a culture of asking questions, giving opinions and challenging the usual way that things are done.
